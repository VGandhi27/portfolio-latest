
About Vidushi Gandhi

👩‍💻 Professional Background

She specializes in detecting configuration changes, developing network dashboards, and creating a Dark Network Operating Center for Airtel. Her expertise lies in network data storage, alarm systems, and analytics, contributing to the transformation of telecom networks into intent-driven autonomous systems.
